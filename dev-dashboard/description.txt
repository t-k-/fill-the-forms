Form Hero author knows how painful it is! He wishes Form Hero can be a helpful hero :)

It is not limited to login names or email addresses, it can help you remember almost any inputs on websites! Forms, blanks, checkboxes, dropdown manuals  and whatever..

And, it is free! Get an easy life!

Typically, you use Form Hero like the following:

Step 1: Click remember all filled values to let Form Hero remember the inputs.

Step 2: When you see this form again, or even a similar form from other websites, you can fill each blanks by a single click on one of the suggested candidates Form Hero gives you. Or apply all with default values, again, with only one click.

Form Hero ranks the top candidate values for you, the very first one for each input is the most likely value Form Hero thinks for that blank.
